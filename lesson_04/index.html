SUDNO

Sudno is a post-punk band that creates a lot of relaxation music. Most of its songs are written on Ukrainian. When you listen to Sudno`s tracks you plunge into a whirlpool of nostalgia, somtimes calmness, sometimes hopeless and every time relaxation.

As for me, SUDNO is the best Ukrainian music band, almost every its song I liked. Nevertheless, I have a favourite one: "City was here(Originally: Тут було місто)"

Linkin Park

Linkin Park is an American rock band formed in Agoura Hills, California, in 1996. The band's current lineup consists of vocalist/rhythm guitarist/keyboardist Mike Shinoda, lead guitarist Brad Delson, DJ/turntablist Joe Hahn, bassist Dave Farrell, co-lead vocalist Emily Armstrong, and drummer Colin Brittain. The lineup for the band's first seven studio albums included lead vocalist Chester Bennington and drummer Rob Bourdon until Bennington's death by suicide in July 2017, which caused the band to enter an indefinite hiatus. In September 2024, Linkin Park's reformation was announced along with the addition of Armstrong and Brittain.
Source:Wikipedia

NAZVA

NAZVA is a Galician-Donbass Ukrainian musical group created in 2019 by Pavel Gotz (Lviv) and Yaroslav Yarovenko (Gorlivka). The style is self-identified as "alternative ethno-art chaos". The band's music is created by playing double bass, viola, keys, and flor tom. They began their creative path as a musical theater, creating performances about the Executed Revival and soundtracks for art exhibitions. Finalists of the Ukrainian National Selection for Eurovision 2024.
Source:Wikipedia

I highly recommend to listen to their album "ГУРМА", the music on this album is both relaxing and melodic, it is worth hearing!
